Frequently Asked Questions

How do tokens work?
1 token = 1 FlipBot conversation OR 1 Area Scan. Tokens are automatically refunded if a scan returns 0 results or encounters an error. You start with 5 free tokens on first launch.
Is my data stored online?
No. All data (inventory, leads, search history) is stored locally on your device. No accounts required. No servers. Your data stays private.
How do I restore purchases?
Token bundles are consumable purchases and cannot be restored. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID (iOS) or Google account (Android) when making purchases.
The Find feature isn't working
Make sure you've granted location permissions to FlipHQ. Go to Settings → FlipHQ → Location → Allow While Using App. Also ensure you have an active internet connection.
FlipBot isn't responding
Check your internet connection. FlipBot requires an active connection to function. Also ensure you have tokens available (1 token per conversation).
How do I delete all my data?
Go to the Home screen → Tap the trash icon (top right) → Confirm "Clear All". This permanently deletes all inventory, leads, and search history. This cannot be undone.
Can I use FlipHQ offline?
Partially. You can view your inventory, leads, and logged items offline. However, the Find feature (location search) and FlipBot (AI chat) require an internet connection.
How accurate are the profit estimates?
Estimates are based on market averages and should be used as guidelines. Actual profit depends on many factors including condition, market demand, timing, and your selling skills. Always do your own research.
